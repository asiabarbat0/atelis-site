How It Works

From upload to purchase —
one guided path to conversion.

Shoppers upload their space. ATELIS analyzes it, surfaces the products most likely to convert, and visualizes them in place — before they buy.

01
Upload Your Space

The shopper photographs their room — or scans a QR code from their phone. No app required. Works on any device, any platform.

02
Receive Curated Recommendations

ATELIS analyzes style, palette, materials, and spatial layout. It surfaces the products most likely to convert — ranked by compatibility.

03
Visualize & Buy

Products render photorealistically into the shopper's actual room. They see exactly how it looks. Hesitation disappears. They buy.
